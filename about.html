Hi! I'm Greg! I'm a doctor (graduate of Lake Erie College of Medicine, Erie, PA) and after being a part of the medical field for 5 years, I've decided to switch into a field where my passions truly lie: into the field of Computer Science! I am currently enrolled in a Masters Program in Computer Science through Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, NJ, with an anticipated graduation date of September 2022. Prior to becoming a Masters student, I've been self-taught, completing many software-related courses offered by University of Duke, Michigan, San Diego, and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, hosted through Coursera. I participated in a 3 month long retreat/workshop at the Recurse Center in Brooklyn, NY, where I built, paired, learned, and collaborated, with a community of software developers of vast, diverse backgrounds and levels of expertise on several projects, such as, OS, game, and web development. I'm currently a Co-Founder of myMedsRec and Poof! API, serving as a Full-Stack JavaScript Developer for both. I am so very excited, happy, and motivated to be a part of the programming community and I look forward to continue learning and improving my software skills and knowledge. I hope to be able to share my skills with the right team and grow alongside a tech company that also shares my enthusiasm for software development.
